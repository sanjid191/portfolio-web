About Project

Stuck juggling grades and semester averages? This website simplifies student life with a user-friendly CGPA calculator. Input your course credits and grades, and it will instantly calculate your semester GPA and overall CGPA. No more manual calculations - this tool keeps you informed of your academic standing and even estimates your graduation class (at some universities). Focus on your studies, let the website handle the number crunching.
